Project Description

"A pink star with eight edges consist of eight kite shape as the spikes. Each tip facing the direction of compas. The one facing the north and south is the longest one. The one facing the east and west is the second longest. The rest facing northwest, northeast, southwest, and southeast with shortest in length. Each of the kite shape is divided by two color, dark pink on the left and bright pink on the right. Under it, there's a spiral with a shape like isosceles triangle with acute angles facing up."
