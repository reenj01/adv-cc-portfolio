6. Data Portrait

Project Description

I realized how much I relied on phones and how I used it as my escape from social interactions as someone who is an introvert and cannot be social all the time. So, I decided to keep track of the time, location, number of people around me, and the application that I open whenever I go on my phone for no specific reason but to avoid social interactions.
